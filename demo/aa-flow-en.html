VISUAL WALKTHROUGH · AA + SESSION KEYS

The magic of no wallet popups during gameplay.
What actually happens under the hood?

Classic Web3 games trigger a signature popup on every click. A Session Account signs just once at deposit, and the main wallet stays out of the way for the rest of the session. Below: (1) the feel of it, (2) the structure, and (3) the actual internal flow — in order.

The three layers of a Session Account

Vault → Locker → Keycard. The metaphor maps one-to-one to three smart contracts.

LAYER 1 · MAIN WALLET

🏦 Vault (EOA)

The user's main wallet. Custodies all funds. Signs only on deposit and withdrawal. Stays idle during gameplay.

0xA1…d3
LAYER 2 · SMART ACCOUNT

📦 Hub (per-game locker)

One smart account per user. Keeps a separate balance slot per game. Verifies session-key signatures and calls the game on the user's behalf.
